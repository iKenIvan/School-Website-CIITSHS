BE A CIITEEN

CIIT Philippines has long been viewed as one of the best schools in the Philippines for Multimedia Arts and Technology.

CIIT introduced Specialist Courses in IT and Digital Arts. Over the years, CIIT was accredited by the Department of Education to offer high school programs in Animation and Computer Science.

Today, CIIT is best known for multimedia arts, 3D animation, web design, and mobile game development. Students learn not only programming and information management, but also information assurance and security and technopreneurship, among others.

We offer a Senior High School program that prepares students to adapt to the industry's changing demands, both now and in the future. We created an industry-standard curriculum that incorporates hands-on learning in multimedia, animation, and programming, and equips students with information and abilities that they can apply even before entering college. Our Senior High School curriculum includes specialized areas that can be used to college credits, especially if a student chooses to continue their education at CIIT.

All of our CIIT SHS graduates found it simple to continue their education at CIIT. They will be automatically accepted into our College programs. They won't have to go through the application process or prepare and submit any paperwork as a result.
